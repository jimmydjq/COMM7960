Hello! I am a researcher and storyteller passionate about exploring how media technologies shape urban life and health communication. With interdisciplinary training in journalism, communication, design, and management, I am particularly interested in human-centered approaches to interactive media design and how communicative practices influence social experience in contemporary cities.

My work bridges urban communication and digital health, examining how media systems, interfaces, and narratives mediate spatial perception, trust, and interaction. At the same time, I explore the use of AI-assisted tools in qualitative research to support data interpretation and analytical rigor.
